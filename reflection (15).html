i got not love greater than myself

love's a certain word for me. an action that should be done.
it means taking care of someone or something that isn't even your obligation.
you're doing things out of kindness even if it doesn't involve you.

i don't have that quality.
i could care less about someone passing an exam because i don't feel obligated to tell the items or questions they're about to face. or even offer a seat that i paid or worked hard to obtain.

much of the love that i carry was just enough for me. i think of myself most of the time. i think that's just right—no one is my responsibility—the same goes for them too.
i carry this love, to protect me from selflessness; in which only i would suffer. i reckon to be even worse because i don't spend much for myself.
