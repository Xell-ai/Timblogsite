i wonder what i truly wanted

when i was child I dreamt of seeing a glimpse of my future, like the movie Meet the Robinsons. i'd think of it in my younger years whenever i feel in despair or hopelessness. And cry because i don't know where any of the things that im doing would eventually lead me to something bigger, brighter than I've expected.

But now, i don't know anymore. I just don't know what im supposed to do. I haven't got a plan for life. The clock is ticking on me. The future doesn't wait for the people who's still deciding. It's just ticks and ticks until it's too late for you get up and go on with your pipe dreams.
