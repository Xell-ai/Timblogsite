[i'm a leech]

i'm a leech. i want to cling onto you
and consume your blood until you
turn into a pulp. or maybe i just
want someone to hold me oh so
dearly. i want to rest on your
shoulders and tell you how my day
was. latch onto you like there's no
tomorrow. intertwined and never
letting go. i'm starving and you're
the bread that feeds my soul. no
gaps, no spaces until we find out
that it's just us together. caress me
as we turn into bones forever.
