what do i want?

as of now, "love" is a sort of fairy tale concept for me. saving someone from a castle against a fire breathing dragon or maybe i was just talking about Shrek.

though, things like that could happen. Us against the world. Or just someone saved me from an embarrassing moment. Where I'm about to cry or is crying in front of everyone. There, there goes my savior—my knight in shining armor and we're going to get the hell out of that gladiator arena.

there's one thing i really like about life, insignificant things that matters to me because to some, it don't. that's what makes my life romantic. Catching up or slowing up their pace of walking just to be beside me. Or waiting for me to tie my shoe up whilst everyone continued their walking. Funny as it may seem, the bare minimum, it gets me.

there's something good about this tiny tender moments that could make me feel like i could take a pause of the world this time.
