[casa]


i want a house i can call a home.
someone who takes the time to peel the white pith of the oranges,
and carefully pick out every bone from the milkfish.
a home that doesn't hurl words at me like sharp blades,
but reassures me when i fidget,
when i spiral into overthinking—
without making me feel dramatic.
i suppose my house doesn't need to be big.
