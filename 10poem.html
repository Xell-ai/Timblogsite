[if it's written, we'll meet again]

if i'm the Author, i would've made it
happen a couple of times—until you
finally understood what it meant. i'd
bend every rule and dictate possibilities
about how our paths could cross.
no matter the adversaries,
the inevitable would always find
its way. in every version of the story,
it is always you my gaze seeks.
not a word spoken. our eyes will meet,
and silence between us becomes
our endless conversation.
