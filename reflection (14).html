to whom it may concern

i don't know you yet. but i hope that you could tolerate the most irrepressible, uncoordinated, and fidgety person you're about to meet. that's me.
may our interests align. and if not, a little compromise could place us on the same wavelength.
just so you know, the most stupid, insignificant things in life are all i care about.
i like taking photos—much better if you'd be in them.

i like writing: about my introspection, death, and quiet intimacy. i'd like to confess—far more than i could ever say aloud. im an open book, but the footnotes are yours to read.

i might not be able to take you to a lot of places, but every time you're with me, it would still feel like an adventure.
in all of this, i'd like to say that i want to be the best of myself when we cross paths. the courageous one—the kind who doesn't let any moments slip through his fingers.
