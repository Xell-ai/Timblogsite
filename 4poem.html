[how's strange?]

i don't know who you are. it's not that
I've forgotten about you—but I don't know
who you are anymore without me, and it kills me.
i'm not saying that you had to stay the same
but I didn't think you'd change. i thought
our time together would bring us closer.
but the distance seemed bigger—and
meaner—than yesterday. you chased after
your dreams, and now i'm in second place.
