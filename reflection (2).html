the afternoon after the incident

i still remember that day, witnessing cirrocumulus clouds at dusk. i was still in disbelief that she was already gone. a cold breeze was in the air but i couldn't feel my self. void is slowly growing inside of me. i couldn't keep a straight face going to school. i could laugh at their jokes but the smile would fade quickly. the sadness that i felt choked me. i could never feel the same way again.
