remembering is more intimate than grand gestures

i find this quite hard to relate to. but if it's done to me, i'd probably soar higher than heavens.
it is challenging for me because i don't even remember every word that had come out of my mouth. i swear, or hypothetically, someone remembers everything that i've said more than i do. even my hand gestures, they keep taunting me albeit i already forgotten that i've done that.

i guess that'd be absolutely romantic for someone, because noticing even the most little detail about a person is better than a hundred statement of 'i love you'.
to be loved is to be seen, being acknowledged for what you really are; of your perfect imperfections. but what matters most is emotional intimacy, because that's what brings attunement to your relationship. i don't even know where im coming from, but i know that i've unknowingly written about attunement before.
it's not that hard to understand. it is when the most insignificant things you care about also matters to the other end. in that way, there's no reason to be bored, grand gestures might sound vague for you but being remembered means you are chosen, even in the smallest moments.
