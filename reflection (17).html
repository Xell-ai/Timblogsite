what does it feel like to be chosen?

my answer is: i don't know
i don't know what's the feeling of being chosen.
in a room full of people, i doubt someone would pick me—even out of pity.
i have never shown them love because i don't know what it was.
in return, they wouldn't consider me either.

should there be a reason to be chosen?
if i was mess, would i be chosen?
there's no point in choosing if it has no reasonable excuse or purpose.
would they choose me even if i had nothing to offer?
i want to be chosen, not considering what i could or couldn't offer. i want to be chosen from all sides of the circle—even if they are bumpy.
