i don't want to become a memory

i want to have strong bonds that last, that will be ingrained in their mind. I want to be someone they think about out of the blue. I want to be unforgettable, the one that unexpectedly surfaces in their thoughts. I want to be the one who randomly comes to mind, a cherished person. I want to be there, or ever-present like I'm everywhere. I'd love to be loved someday but I don't want to be forgotten like a gravestone in a cemetery that will eventually grow mold and never cleaned since the day it was buried there. I don't want to be six-feet underground that is mixed in soil that no one recognizes. I don't want to be in a nameless grave. I want to be known. I want to be etched deep in their skin like a tattoo that they'll have forever on their chest. I long to be in their mind rent-free or revived in their thoughts that I was still there. To be remembered that I was kind to them or the one who listens or just someone that was beside through thick and thin. Maybe this is my personal anecdote or what I'm really yearning for or just another dumb thought that came to mind....
