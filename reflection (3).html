if not love, what is it that i desire?

i don't think that there's anything that doesn't fall under the umbrella of love. I'd like to be specific here, but i think it's hope. hope that never falter. hope that never cease. no matter how hard the predicament, it's still there. it's good enough to think that there's always a way things outof everything. i've tried to avoid material things because it's not permanent.

it's an object, that is temporary, that probably won't last long. definitely would give me joy at one point, but i know it wouldn't last. i truly believe that the intangibles are eternal. ideas, emotions, concepts and hope is eternal. it transcends time and it never goes out of style no matter how the society evolves.
