[05102024] freedom

I've thought about this topic many days ago (05082024) but now's the only time to fully engage into it.

A question at first,
What is freedom?
According to Google freedom is..
"the power or right to act, speak, or think as one wants without hindrance or restraint."

According to philosophy, freedom is...
"Freedom is the power of a sentient being to exercise its will"

Personally, I think freedom is the thing we're lucky that were born with and that is promoted by the laws we have. It's a power that the country has given us, the power to choose your clothing, the power to write, power to choose your partner, and the power to express yourself.

But what if this freedom is too much or abused? What will happen ?

Simply, chaos ensues.
It's always bad to have something that is too much for you to take in.

So, the reason I was discussing this because I randomly remembered a quote and I came across a video of Prince, my favorite pop jazz musician giving an Induction speech at the 19th Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2004. In his speech, there's a line that made an impact to me about freedom. "Too much freedom can lead to the soul’s decay".

Taken out of context, it was very powerful line with a meaningful message and it's something that is very logical or something that makes sense because we're in a world or age wherein freedom is a power widely given to almost every person in most country. But when people sometimes have this too much or dose in acquiring independence and it would turn into making bad decisions as freedom allows us to do so. It would sometimes lead to kill you, because the opportunity to eat many as you are allowed to, can lead to obesity, diabetes, heart attack, strokes and eventually death. Having the opportunity to smoke can cost you your life. Having the opportunity to drink all you want can also cost you your life. A freedom in choosing an unhealthy can also be detrimental to your life. And, having this much freedom can also cause harm or interfere to other people's freedom. I'm narrating this because I've been seeing a lot issues that is caused by having too much freedom. Especially our rights to exercise freedom of speech or expression mostly on social media wherein we could say what we want to say but sometimes it isn't just good for people to have much freedom. I mostly see this in western media , because their ideologies and their actions influences the whole world and some of their ideologies doesn't apply or align to the customs of other countries which would taken as offensive or rude. But for some reason, some people can see this in media and it influences them to mimic that action thinking that it is rightful to do but actually isn't.

You see, this freedom is a power that created by humans or society or I can say that it is something God has given us, that taught us to have; the power to choose. Acquiring this power also holds a responsibility and consequence kills. The solution is, to have awareness that we have the liability in acquiring it. This solution offers us to finally have authority over our freedom knowing that it would lead to such consequences. The main point is, our freedom shouldn't tamper or infringe with other people's freedom in order to have balance in our society. Also to create this balance, there should be an "order", laws, rules and regulations to abide by. Like a doctor who would prescribe you such medicines and teaches you directions to better yourself and to finally escape the illness you're suffering and you're supposed to follow the doctor's advice if you want to save yourself,take this or take that, eat this or avoid. You're easily persuaded to follow the things the doctor's advice because you want to save yourself from further danger. Lastly,sometimes it isn't about having too much freedom but it's about the awareness of our responsibility and having the morality and self-restraint over our actions.
