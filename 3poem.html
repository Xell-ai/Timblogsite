[i want to know you]

i'd like to know more about you. i want
to pry your head open and see if I ever
crossed your mind. unravel you like the
forbidden Pandora's box. i love the
unknown—and you're the mystery I ache
to uncover. seek the truth that only
I would know. i want to read you like
a book, chapter by chapter. the
nooks, the crannies, the things others
overlook. you're a progressive
piece of art that i long to be
part of.
