[i can create you into a thousand poems but i can't make you love me. ]

just why?
i thought words are powerful
but you remained stagnant.
unmoved.

i carved you into metaphors,
wrote you syllables and sighs
yet you stood silent—
of my unimaginable cries
you're deaf and petrified.

how much more ink
should i bleed?
as my yearning for you
refuses to die.

you're still not looking,
i'm guessing you're also blind.
i reckon love,
is one that language cannot conjure
