can life never be fulfilled?

when i was a child, i thought getting something could complete your life. but it wasn't, and i was wrong. i still kept that attitude to this day. i thought that getting a toy, buying a new furniture or a new shirt could finally make me happy. yes, i was happy but only for a while. the dopamine that it gives me doesn't last long. i would always want, thinking it would be the answer.
yet, i was never satisfied. i thought that getting a new shoes, a music player, or a new camera could finally complete me. but it still wasn't enough. i still see flaws in every aspect of my life. my lightbulb is worn out, my desk is messy, the never-ending pile of laundry and the uninviting appearance of my room. there's always something missing. i could see what i want but not face the small problems in front of me.
