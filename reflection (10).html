organic encounter who?

i think i know who i wanna meet in the next four years.
maybe one day in a bookstore, both of us reaching a book that caught our eye. nothing grandiose, nothing cinematic—just real. maybe during a traffic light where i would glance back many times. on a random stroll around the park, where I'd see her from a distance and instinctively drift away, afraid to be seen. Or on a jeepney, two people with different errands in mind and places we're going to—yet, in the same space.

Will I grab the chance to start a conversation with her? Or shy away until my chances ran out?
I'm certain that I still wanna meet her in any place. A glimpse, a gaze, a face that I can still remember.
And when that day arrives I hope that I'm best version of myself when I had the chance to meet her again—the guts to conquer the distance that existed between us over the years.

Possibilities are never-ending—too may for anyone to hold all at once. Maybe i'll open another bright chapter or just another passing encounter...
