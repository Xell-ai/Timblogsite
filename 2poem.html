[it's you]

it's always you. only you who appears to
take my troubles away. you make it seem
perfect even when everything was flawed.
my refuge when the world is in shambles. the
steady breath when i feel uncertain.
enchanted by your ways—and i simply can't get
enough of your taste. it's addicting that I
would always want more, that I'd be dying
without you holding me.
