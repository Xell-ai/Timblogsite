too much music kills

music can be such a great escape from these misery that we experience in this lifetime. we can rely into it to relieve ourselves—it could even help us hasten our work, be motivated and push more creative ventures. but my mantra says otherwise, having too much kills you. this goes the same in listening to music or relying on it too much. i admit that having your own playlist or curated genre that you could listen whenever you want to is actually addicting.

but having to much reliance on music can turn into a real concern. it's like an opium for your head and brain; you can't work without it, can't eat without it and you specially can't think straight without it. like come on, face your world, listen to it's natural chaos instead of silencing them with music. i saw it as a concern because i encountered problems more than it benefited me. yes, it is comforting that i could listen wherever i go but it also held responsibilities for me. i, as a person, i'm quite lacking in spatial awareness; with music, im completely devoid of the world around me. whenever i'm stressed or on my way home, im used to listen to music; without, my emotions are heightened, i could feel much frustration and existential dread combined with a burnout from stress. it is my way to cope, this is like smoking for me but im saying that what im having is much worse. or im probably just saying this from an inexperienced tongue.

but come on, you have to face what's in front of you. you don't always have to rely on music to solve your problems. you're not in a musical film or MV that miraculously makes it out by listening to music. music is actually a distraction from the situations that we actually have to face head on. one must face occurring challenges without any form of distraction. do it scared,do it inexperienced, do it bored, and most of all, do it for yourself. our selves is the only key to get out of these problems—getting out of the comfort zone is the sole answer.

[this is my personal reflective essay after watching All About Lilly Chou-chou]
